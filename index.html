Why Construction Management System

For Managers

Managers can keep the projects on track. Using the dashboard in this Management System can help keeping track of finance, budget, schedules, raw materials, specifications, and more.

Read more

For Employees

Employees can enter details such as completed work, budget, material, control, manpower and more. They can keep track of all the work they need to handle within the given time to keep the projects on track.

Read more

For Suppliers and Vendors

Suppliers and vendors can check if there are enough materials required for the project. They will be notified if more materials are requried, and can simultaneously update in the Construction Management Dashboard.
